OUR STORY

Haven Apartment was founded by four individuals—Eric, Dave, Gerald, and Junn—who shared one vision: to create a place where people can feel safe, comfortable, and truly at home. The idea began during a simple conversation over coffee. The four noticed how many students, workers, and families struggled to find a peaceful and reliable place to stay. Most accommodations offered space, but not comfort—rooms, but not a sense of home.

Driven by this realization, they asked themselves a question: What if we built a place that feels like a peaceful refuge—a haven? With that shared dream, Haven Apartment was born. Starting with determination and a commitment to quality, they created a space designed not just for living, but for resting, growing, and finding comfort. Today, Haven Apartment stands as a reflection of their mission: to offer a welcoming environment where every resident feels valued and at ease.
